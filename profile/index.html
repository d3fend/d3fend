Warning: Clearing your browser cookies will delete all of your data saved in this application.

Localstorage is a feature of your browser that allows websites and applications to store data on your computer. This data is saved even after you close the browser or restart your computer.

This application uses localstorage to save your data so that you can access it quickly and easily next time you use the application. However, if you clear your browser cookies, all of your data saved in this application will be lost.

If you need to clear your browser cookies, be sure to back up your data first. You can do this by exporting your data from the application or by manually copying and pasting it into a safe location.

To back up your data:

  1. Go to the /profile/ or the /profile-view/ page.
  2. Click on the "Export Data" button.
  3. Save the exported file to a safe location.

To restore your data:

  1. go to the /profile/ or the /profile-view/ page.
  2. Click on the "Import Data" button.
  3. Select the previously exported file.
  4. Click "Import"

Please note: If you do not back up your data before clearing your browser cookies, all of your data will be lost and cannot be recovered.
