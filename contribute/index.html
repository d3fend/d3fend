Contribute to D3FEND

D3FEND is an early stage initiative and needs community input to grow. We welcome any feedback or contributions to the project.

There are numerous ways to connect with the D3FEND team or submit contributions or feedback.

  • D3FEND Github
  • D3FEND Slack
  • Email d3fend@mitre.org

Contribution Processes:

Are you interested in contributing to the next version of D3FEND?

  • Is there a defensive technique or digital artifact you want added?
  • Are there missing or incorrect relationships?
  • Does a knowledgebase article seem incomplete, dated, or inaccurate?
